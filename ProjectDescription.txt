Project Vision and Demographics

Vision:

Summary: This app is for people who seem to always be looking for others to make decisions for them. Maybe you wake up one morning
and just can't decide which shirt matches your pants best. Maybe you want opinions on what picture to post on Instagram or even you're stuck
in between names for a new pet, Committii will give you the decisiveness you need.

Project Idea: A social media site dedicated to polling.
Name: “Committii”
Slogan: "Life by committee"
Technical points of focus: Charts (Pie and Bar), Polling, Result Timing
Users: Individuals who vote on polls called committees/committers

Demographics:

Sex: Male or Female
Age: 14 - 45
Adjectives: Indecisive, Impressionable, Influential, Curious
